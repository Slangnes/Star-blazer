🌌 How to Play StarBlazer

Objective

Surround the opponent's Royal (♛) chip completely with other pieces (occupied spaces or blocked slide paths) so that it cannot move.

General Rules

  • First Placement: The very first chip must go to the center of the board.
  • Subsequent Placements: New reserve pieces must touch at least one of your own chips, and must not touch any opponent chips.
  • One-Field Rule: Once on the board, pieces can move, but the entire group of chips (the field) must always remain connected.
  • Royal Placement: You must place your Royal chip on the board during one of your first 4 turns.

Meet the Pieces

♛
Royal: Moves exactly 1 space. Keep it safe!
🛡
Soldier: Slides any distance around the outer perimeter of the field.
⎈
Corvette: Moves 1 space. Can climb on top of other chips and walk across stacked pieces.
⬦
Hopper: Jumps in a straight line over any number of chips to the first empty space.
★
General: Slides along the outer perimeter up to 3 spaces.
